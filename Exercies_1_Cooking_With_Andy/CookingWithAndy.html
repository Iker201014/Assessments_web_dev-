Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious homemade cookies with gooey chocolate chips!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

If you don't have eggs, you can use applesauce, mashed bananas, or yogurt. Use 1/4 cup per egg.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C.
  2. Mix flour and baking soda.
  3. Cream butter, sugar, and vanilla.
  4. Add eggs one at a time.
  5. Add dry mixture.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop onto baking sheet.
  8. Bake 8-10 minutes.
  9. Cool on rack.
